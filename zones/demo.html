Static two-player rules demo · 7×7 board · Ventrix vs Gemsdig

Ending rule: 15 rounds
Hot-zone rule: Hot zones generated every 5 rounds
Base Action mod: No reservations allowed

Redeem ZONE card

Click Redeem, then choose one public ZONE card from the ZONE card pool.

Action 1: Occupy

Click 0 to 3 empty cells. Power spent equals the number of occupied tiles.

Action 2: Tilt

Click at most 1 tile from each enemy and convert it to your faction. Power spent: 2.

Turn control

You must complete exactly one action each turn. Ties in power keep the previous relative order.
