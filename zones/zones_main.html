A board game blending strategic tile placement with card mechanics

Overview

ZONES is a board game I designed on a 10×10 board (If by default 4 players are playing). Players use cards to place, remove, and transform tiles in order to build formations and maximize scores.

Core Rules

  • Four eras of the game
  • Pick 2 + Draft 4 to form your 6-card hand
  • Compete on hot-zone tiles (they score points at the end of each era)
  • Form shapes to redeem ZONES cards (they give great score bonuses)
  • Play cards to:
    • Occupy the board (your occupied tiles scores after 4 eras)
    • Tilt or remove others (which is the core fun for most board games)
    • Conduct special effects or direct score points (where the amount likely to depend on your tile locations on the board)
