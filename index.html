Yiheng Shen 沈毅恒

Ph.D. Candidate, Duke University

I am currently a fifth-year Ph.D. student at Duke University. I am very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Kamesh Munagala. My research interests lie in the interface of economics and computation, especially fairness and stability in resource allocation problems.

I was a student researcher at Google Research, hosted by Aaron Schild in the Summer of 2023. I interned at Meta in the Summer of 2025, supervised by Wenshun Liu. Prior to joining Duke, I received my bachelor's degree at Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences (Yao Class), Tsinghua University.
